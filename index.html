Controls
  • Change elevation with Up/Down arrows
  • Turn with Left/Right arrows
  • Move forward/backward with I/K
  • Strafe left with J and right with L or M
  • Roll left/right with U/O
  • Zoom in/out with N/W
  • Pitch up/down with ;/P
